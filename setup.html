ADHD Protocol Setup

Welcome to the ADHD Protocol dashboard. This tool is designed to interface directly with your local Obsidian vault to help manage focus, tasks, and daily logs.

ðŸ”Œ Obsidian Integration

To use this dashboard effectively, you need to connect it to your Obsidian Vault.

1. Obsidian Setup

Ensure you have Obsidian installed and running with your desired vault open and the below plugins installed.

2. Vault Name

In the main dashboard (adhd-protocol.html), scroll to the bottom of the "Protocol Controls" card. Enter your generic Obsidian Vault name in the "Vault Name" field. This is required for the URL schemes to work.

This dashboard heavily relies on the following community plugins. Please search for and install them from the Obsidian Community Plugins settings.

Optional but recommended also consider donating to the authors of these plugins as my work would be impossible without their work.

  • Advanced URI: Essential. Allows this dashboard to command Obsidian to create files, open headers, and perform complex actions. Donate to vinzent.
  • Dataview: Required for the dashboard templates to dynamically query your vault (e.g., showing inbox items or "Did" lists). Donate to blacksmithgu.
  • Tasks: Used in the daily notes to filter and display tasks from across your vault. Donate to claremacrae.
